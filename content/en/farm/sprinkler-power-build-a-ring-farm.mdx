<Section icon={Sun} title="Optimizing Your Sprinkler Power Upgrade Path">
To maximize your farm's potential, a strategic approach to upgrading **Sprinkler Power** is essential. It's not just about pouring money into it, but understanding its place in your overall progression.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Seed Luck Investment">
    Begin by focusing on "Upgrade Seed Luck." This helps you acquire rarer seeds (Epic, Legendary, Exotic, Transcended) which have a much higher base sale value. Without good seeds, even the fastest growth won't yield optimal profits.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Farm Expansion & Basic Sprinkler">
    Once you have a few rare seeds, expand your farm to accommodate more plots. Ensure you have a basic sprinkler system in place to water all your current crops. At this stage, focus on "Sprinkler Range" to cover all your plots efficiently.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Prioritize Sprinkler Power">
    With a good selection of high-value seeds and a sufficiently sized farm, start heavily investing in **Sprinkler Power**. This will drastically reduce the growth time of your valuable crops, allowing for more frequent harvests.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Balance with Saw Yield">
    As Sprinkler Power increases growth speed, ensure your "Saw Yield" is also upgraded. This ensures that when your crops are ready, you're getting the maximum possible harvest from them. A balanced approach between growth speed and harvest quantity is key.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Continuous Optimization">
    Periodically review your farm's performance. If crops are growing faster than they are being harvested, increase Saw Yield. If harvesting is quick but growth is slow, further boost **Sprinkler Power**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Don't Neglect Saw Yield">
A common mistake is to over-invest in Sprinkler Power without adequately upgrading Saw Yield. While fast growth is good, if your saw isn't efficiently collecting the bounty, you're leaving money on the table. Both work in tandem for maximum profit.
</Callout>

This iterative process ensures you're always making the most impactful upgrades for your current farm state.

</Section>
