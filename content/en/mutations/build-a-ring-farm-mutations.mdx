<Section icon={Droplets} title="How to Obtain Mutations: Events vs. Sprays">

There are two primary ways to acquire **build a ring farm mutations**: through natural weather events or by purchasing sprays from the Gear Shop. Events are luck-based but free, while sprays offer a guaranteed result at a high monetary cost.

### Weather Events and Chances
When a mutation event occurs, every plant currently placed on your farm gets a "roll" to see if it mutates. Some events offer 1 to 3 rolls per plant, significantly increasing your chances if you have a full field.

| Event Name | Associated Mutation | Roll Chance | Price of Spray |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Rain** | Wet | 8% | $10,000,000 |
| **Blizzard** | Frozen | 4% | $750,000,000 |
| **Black Hole** | Void | 3% | $1,000,000,000 |
| **Nuclear** | Radioactive | 2% | $10,000,000,000 |
| **Galaxy** | Rainbow | 1% | $1,000,000,000,000 |

  <Step num="1" title="Check the Weather">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Fill Your Plots">
    Before the event ends, ensure every single one of your farming plots is occupied by a plant. The more plants you have, the higher the statistical probability that at least one will mutate.

  <Step num="3" title="Manual Application">

</Steps>
