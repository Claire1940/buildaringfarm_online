<Section icon={Target} title="Strategies for Acquiring Mutations">
Obtaining mutations can be a game-changer for your farm's profitability. There are two primary methods to acquire them: through random in-game events or by purchasing specialized sprays from the Gear Shop.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Monitor In-Game Events">
    Keep an eye on the in-game event timer. Each mutation is tied to a specific event (e.g., Rain Event for Wet, Galaxy Event for Rainbow). During these events, there's a small chance your plants will spontaneously mutate. You must be online and actively playing during the event for this chance to occur.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Purchase Mutation Sprays">
    If you have sufficient funds, you can buy specific mutation sprays from the Gear Shop. These sprays allow you to apply a desired mutation directly to a plant without relying on event randomness. The cost varies significantly, with powerful mutations like Rainbow Spray costing up to $1 Trillion.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Strategic Application">
    Once you have a spray or a plant mutates, apply it to a high-rarity, high-level plant to maximize the income boost. Since mutations don't stack, focus on giving each of your best plants its own powerful mutation.
  </Step>
</Steps>
