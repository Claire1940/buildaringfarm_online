<Section icon={Target} title="The Core Mechanic: Understanding the Mutation Limit">

In the competitive world of Roblox farming simulators, maximizing your income per second is the difference between a struggling garden and a multi-trillion dollar empire. While high-rarity seeds and level-ups provide a solid foundation, mutations are the true endgame multipliers. However, many players often wonder about the stacking potential of these buffs.

The fundamental rule of the game is simple: **a single seed can only hold one mutation at any given time.** There is no way to stack a "Wet" mutation with a "Radioactive" one to get a combined multiplier. If your plant is already mutated and a new weather event occurs, that plant is ineligible for a new mutation unless the previous one is manually removed.

<Callout type="warning" title="Anti-Stacking Warning">
Do not waste your expensive Sprays trying to use them on a plant that already has a mutation. The game will not allow the new effect to take hold, and you may lose the resource without gaining the benefit. Always check the visual VFX of your plant before applying a new Spray.
</Callout>

### Why the One-Mutation Limit Matters
This limitation forces players to make strategic choices. Early in the game, you might settle for a Wet mutation because it is easy to get during Rain events. However, as you progress toward the late game, you will want to clear those lower-tier buffs to make room for the 5x Rainbow multiplier. This creates a gameplay loop of upgrading and refining your farm's efficiency.

| Feature | Limitation Details |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Max Mutations** | **1 per single seed/plant** |
| **Stacking** | **Not possible** |
| **Replacement** | **Requires Acid Spray removal** |
| **VFX Priority** | **Current mutation dictates appearance** |

<Section icon={Zap} title="How to Acquire Mutations: Events vs. Sprays">

There are two primary ways to mutate your plants in Build a Ring Farm. The first is passive and relies on RNG (Random Number Generation) during specific weather events. The second is active and involves purchasing specialized Sprays from the Gear Shop.

### Method 1: Weather Events
Weather events occur periodically while you are in the game. Each event corresponds to a specific mutation. If you are active during these events, every non-mutated plant on your farm has a small percentage chance to transform.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Stay Online">
    Mutations only trigger while you are actively in the game session. You cannot gain mutations while offline.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Trigger the Event">
    Wait for a specific weather notification (e.g., "A Galaxy Event has started!").
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Check Your Plants">
    Once the event ends, scan your farm to see which seeds have successfully mutated.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Method 2: Gear Shop Sprays
For those who prefer a guaranteed result over RNG, the Gear Shop offers Sprays. These are one-time use items that apply a specific mutation to a plant immediately. However, the costs are exponential.

| **Wet Spray** | **$10 Million** | **100%** |
| **Frozen Spray** | **$750 Million** | **100%** |
| **Void Spray** | **$1 Billion** | **100%** |
| **Radioactive Spray** | **$10 Billion** | **100%** |

<Callout type="success" title="Investment Strategy">

</Callout>
