Acquiring the **build a ring farm best mutation** requires either patience for server events or a significant investment in sprays from the **Gear Shop**. Events occur every 10 to 15 minutes, and being online during these windows is essential for free progression.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Plant Your Best Seeds">
    Mutations cannot be applied to seeds in your inventory. You must plant your rarest seeds (like the Horned Melon or Prismatic variants) and let them grow fully before they can receive a mutation from an event.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Monitor the Event Timer">
    Watch the countdown at the top of your screen. When an event like the **Galaxy Event** or **Queen Bee Event** begins, there is a small percentage chance that every planted crop on your farm will spontaneously mutate.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Utilize the Gear Shop">
    If you have the cash, visit the Gear Shop to buy specific sprays. Equipping a spray and clicking on a crop will guarantee that mutation. This is the most reliable way to get **Radioactive** or **Void** effects without waiting for RNG.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Refine with Acid Spray">
    If a low-tier mutation (like Wet) is applied to a high-value plant, use an **Acid Spray** (costing 1 million Cash) to remove it. This clears the slot so you can attempt to get a **Honeycomb** or **Rainbow** mutation instead.
  </Step>
</Steps>
