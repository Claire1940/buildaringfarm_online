### The Outer-Ring Layout Strategy

Seed placement significantly impacts your harvest value due to the ring multiplier system. Crops in the Outer Ring receive a substantial 19x harvest multiplier, compared to 13x in the Middle Ring and 7x in the Base Ring.

This tiered approach ensures that your most valuable crops benefit from the largest multipliers, drastically increasing your overall farm income.

### Upgrading Crops and Using Fertilizers

Once you have planted rare crops, further enhance their value by upgrading them. Select a plot with a crop and hit the "Upgrade" button to increase its sale value. Prioritize upgrading mutated fruits or vegetables, as they yield even higher profits.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Plant High-Rarity Seeds">
    Focus on Secret, Exotic, and Transcended crops to occupy the majority of your plots. While expensive, these seeds can also be obtained from packs like the Tropical Seed Pack.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Upgrade Your Crops">
    After planting, select the crop plot and use cash to upgrade it. Prioritize mutated crops for maximum returns, as they sell for more. A crop can be upgraded to Level 100.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Apply Mutation Sprays & Fertilizers">
    Invest in fertilizers to boost crop growth speed and mutation sprays to increase their sale value. These can be purchased from the Gear Shop.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Expand Your Farm (Later)">
    Farm expansions should be a lower priority initially. Focus on acquiring rare seeds, upgrades, and yield-enhancing items first. Expand only when you have a strong income base.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Maximizing Earnings with Environmental Mutations

Mutations multiply your harvest value, stacking with ring multipliers and seed level scaling. For example, a Wet mutation provides a 1.5x multiplier, while a Honeycomb mutation offers 6.5x.

<Callout type="success" title="Timing Your Harvests">
Time your harvests to coincide with weather events. During a Nuclear event (3x mutation chance) or Galaxy event (5x mutation chance), harvesting your highest-level outer-ring crops will yield significantly more income.
</Callout>
