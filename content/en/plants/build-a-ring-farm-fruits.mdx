<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Farming Optimization">

To maximize your earnings from **build a ring farm fruits**, you need a systematic approach to growth. Simply planting seeds is not enough; you must leverage the game's upgrade and mutation systems.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Indexing">
    Spend your first $100,000 on low-cost Seed Rolls. Your goal is to unlock the Index entries for all Common and Uncommon fruits. This grants a baseline boost to your future rolls.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Epic Transition">
    Once you have a full ring of Rare fruits, stop expanding your farm. Use all your income to target Epic seeds. A single Citrus or Sunflower at Level 20 will outperform your entire starter ring.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Plot Leveling Focus">
    Select your highest-rarity fruit and dump all spare cash into its "Upgrade" button. Each level increases the sale value. Aim for Level 100 on your top 3 earners before upgrading lower-tier plots.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Mutation and Fertilizer Application">
    Visit the Gear Shop. Purchase Mutation Sprays to transform your standard fruits into Mutated versions (which sell for significantly more). Use Fertilizers to reduce grow times, effectively increasing your "Cash Per Second" (CPS).
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Late-Game Expansion">
    Only after you have a stable income of several million per second should you prioritize unlocking the outer rings of your farm. The cost of unlocking new plots scales aggressively.
  </Step>
</Steps>
