An efficient farm layout is paramount for maximizing your income from **build a ring farm exotic seeds**. The goal is to minimize travel time for your harvesters and maximize the density of high-income crops.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Strategic Placement of Harvesters">
    Place your harvesters in the center of your most valuable exotic seed plots. This ensures they spend less time moving and more time collecting. Consider using multiple harvesters for larger plots of high-income seeds.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Grouping Similar Grow Times">
    Group seeds with similar grow times together. This allows for more synchronized harvesting cycles, reducing downtime and optimizing the use of your harvesters. For example, place **Kiwi** (54s) and **Dragonfruit** (55s) in adjacent plots.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Utilizing Space Efficiently">
    Design your farm to utilize every available tile. Avoid empty spaces and create a grid-like structure that allows for easy expansion as you acquire more exotic seeds. Consider a circular or square layout around a central collection point.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Upgrade Facilities">
    Invest in upgrading your seed planters and storage facilities. Faster planters reduce the time it takes to plant new seeds, and larger storage capacity means you can accumulate more produce before selling, leading to fewer trips to the market.
  </Step>
</Steps>
