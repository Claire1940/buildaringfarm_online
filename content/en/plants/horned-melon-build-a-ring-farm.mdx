<Section icon={Target} title="Seed Collector Machine Strategy">
The primary method for obtaining the Horned Melon seed is through the Seed Collector machine, located at the center of the map. This machine rewards players for sacrificing other seeds, accumulating points towards a featured prize.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Locate the Machine">
    Find the Seed Collector machine in the central area of your *Build a Ring Farm* map. It's a prominent structure designed for seed submissions.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Submit Seeds for Points">
    Equip any seed from your inventory and interact with the machine to submit it. Seeds of higher rarity, mutations, and levels will grant significantly more points. Common and Uncommon seeds can still be submitted, but progress will be slower.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Reach Point Threshold">
    Accumulate a total of **180,000 points**. There is a daily cap of **60,000 points**, meaning it will take a minimum of three days to reach the full requirement if you hit the daily limit each time.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Claim Your Reward">
    Once you reach 180,000 points, the Horned Melon seed, if it's the current featured reward, will be granted to you. After receiving the reward, the machine resets to zero, allowing you to start collecting points for future weekly rewards.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Weekly Rotation Alert">
The featured seed reward from the Seed Collector machine changes **every week**. If the **Horned Melon seed** is currently available, prioritize farming points as quickly as possible before the weekly reset to avoid missing out.
</Callout>

</Section>
