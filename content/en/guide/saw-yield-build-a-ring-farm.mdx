<Section icon={Sparkles} title="How Saw Yield Boosts Your Income">
The formula for your total income in Build a Ring Farm clearly illustrates the importance of **Saw Yield**. Each level of Saw Yield upgrade provides a +2x multiplier to your base income. This bonus stacks multiplicatively with your ring multiplier and any mutation multipliers on your plants. This means that a high Saw Yield can turn a good harvest into an incredible one.

Here's a breakdown of how the multipliers combine:

| Multiplier Type   | Effect on Income                                     | Acquisition Method                               |
| :---------------- | :--------------------------------------------------- | :----------------------------------------------- |
| **Base Income**   | Starting value of your crops                         | Crop rarity and level                            |
| **Ring Multiplier** | Determined by your farm's ring (Inner, Middle, Outer) | Unlock through farm expansion                    |
| **Saw Multiplier** | **+2x per level of Saw Yield upgrade**             | Upgrade board                                    |
| **Mutation Multiplier** | Bonus from plant mutations (e.g., Rainbow, Void) | Gear Shop sprays, weather events                 |
| **Total Income**  | Base Income × Ring × Saw × Mutation                  | Combination of all factors                       |

<Section icon={Swords} title="Strategic Saw Yield Upgrades">
Upgrading your **Saw Yield** is a straightforward process, but knowing when and how much to invest can make a significant difference in your farm's profitability. It's one of several upgrades available on the upgrade board, each contributing to different aspects of your farm's efficiency.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Access the Upgrade Board">
    Locate the upgrade board in your base. This board displays various options for enhancing your farm's capabilities.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Identify Saw Yield Upgrade">
    Find the "Saw Yield" option on the board. Its description will clearly state that it increases the yield made by the saw.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Invest Cash">
    Spend your accumulated Cash (in-game currency) to purchase levels of the Saw Yield upgrade. Each purchase will increase your saw's multiplier.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Monitor Income Gains">
    Observe your income per second after each upgrade. You should see a noticeable increase, especially when combined with other multipliers.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Cost Escalation">
Be aware that the cost of upgrades, including **Saw Yield**, will increase significantly as you progress. Plan your investments carefully to maintain a steady flow of income while upgrading. Sometimes, saving for a major farm expansion or a rare seed might be more beneficial than continuously upgrading a lower-tier Saw Yield.
</Callout>

</Section>
