<Section icon={Target} title="Understanding Saw Yield and Harvesting Mechanics">

In Build a Ring Farm, your primary source of income is the rotating saw that circles your garden plots. Many new players confuse the different saw upgrades, but understanding **what saw yield does** is the key to transitioning from thousands to billions of cash per second. Unlike Saw Range, which simply allows the blade to reach further plots, Saw Yield directly impacts the "output" of the harvest.

When the saw passes over a matured plant, it "cuts" the crop and packages it into a box. The value of this box is determined by the base rarity of the seed, the current upgrade level of that specific plot, and your global Saw Yield multiplier. By investing in this stat at the upgrade board, you effectively raise the floor of your income across the entire farm.

<Callout type="tip" title="Efficiency Tip">
Always balance your Saw Yield with your seed rarity. There is no point in having a 10x yield multiplier if you are still harvesting Common Cabbages. Aim to have at least Rare or Epic seeds before dumping all your cash into yield upgrades.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Map} title="Saw Yield vs. Saw Range: Which to Upgrade First?">

Deciding where to spend your hard-earned cash on the upgrade board can be daunting. In the 2026 meta of Build a Ring Farm, the consensus among top-tier players is a "Yield-First" approach. This is because expanding your farm (Saw Range) increases your overhead costs—you have to buy more seeds and pay for more individual plot upgrades.

**Saw Yield** allows you to make more money from the same number of plants. This is significantly more efficient for players who haven't unlocked the second or third floors yet. Once your current plots are generating enough cash to comfortably buy Exotic seeds, then you should focus on **Saw Range** to bring more plants into the rotation.

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Maximizing Yield with Mutations and Sprays">

Saw Yield doesn't work in a vacuum. To truly see "admin abuse" levels of cash, you need to combine your yield upgrades with **Mutation Sprays** from the Gear Shop. Mutations provide a secondary multiplier that sits on top of your Saw Yield. For example, a Rainbow Mutation can boost a crop's value by several thousand percent.

In the current version of the game, the Gear Shop restocks every five minutes. You should always keep a stockpile of **Void Sprays** and **Rainbow Sprays**. If a plant has a weak mutation (like Wet or Frozen), use an **Acid Spray** to remove it and try again for a higher-tier multiplier.

| Mutation Spray | Cash Cost | Effect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Acid Spray** | 1M | Removes current mutation |
| **Wet Spray** | 10M | Applies Wet mutation (Low Multiplier) |
| **Frozen Spray** | 750M | Applies Frozen mutation (Mid Multiplier) |
| **Void Spray** | 10B | Applies Void mutation (High Multiplier) |
| **Rainbow Spray** | 1T | Applies Rainbow mutation (Godly Multiplier) |

<Callout type="success" title="The Trillionaire Secret">
The **Rainbow Spray** is currently the strongest item in the Gear Shop. While it costs 1 Trillion cash, applying it to a high-level **Ghost Pepper** or **Queen Blossom** with maxed Saw Yield can generate over 4 Billion dollars per second.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Step-by-Step Profit Optimization Strategy">

If you want to turn your farm into a money-printing machine, follow this specific progression path. This sequence ensures that your Saw Yield is always supported by the right crops and fertilizers.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Luck Push">
    Spend your first few millions on **Seed Luck** and **Seed Rerolls**. You cannot leverage Saw Yield effectively until you are consistently rolling Epic or Legendary seeds.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Yield Spike">
    Once you have a full row of Legendary crops, dump all your cash into **Saw Yield** until the cost of the next upgrade exceeds your 10-minute earnings.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Mutation Fishing">
    Visit the Gear Shop and buy **Void Sprays**. Apply them to your highest-rarity crops. This creates a massive synergy with your Saw Yield.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Expansion & Range">
    Now that your income is stable, buy the **Farm Expansion** and increase **Saw Range** to include the new plots. Immediately fill these with your best seeds.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Level 100 Grind">
    Manually upgrade each plot to **Level 100**. The combination of Level 100 + Rainbow Mutation + Max Saw Yield is the current "end-game" state for 2026.
  </Step>
</Steps>

</Callout>
