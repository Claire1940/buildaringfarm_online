<Callout type="warning" title="Mutation Event Timing">
Mutation Events are unpredictable weather phenomena that offer a chance for your plants to mutate. Each event provides 1-3 rolls, and every placed plant has a chance to mutate. For example, a Black Hole event gives each plant a 3% chance to mutate into Void. Pay close attention to these events and ensure your farm is ready to maximize mutation chances.
