<Section icon={Target} title="Strategies for Acquiring Mutations">
Obtaining mutations in Build a Ring Farm involves a mix of luck during world events and strategic spending on sprays. Your approach should change as you progress in the game.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Early Game Focus: Wet Mutations">
    For new players, focus on the **Wet mutation**. Rain events happen frequently, offering an 8% chance for crops to mutate. The Wet Spray is also relatively cheap at $10 million, making it accessible for early income boosts. This is a reliable way to start building your mutated crop collection and understand the system.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Mid-Game Progression: Void and Radioactive">
    As your farm grows and you accumulate more wealth, start aiming for **Void** and **Radioactive mutations**. These offer significantly higher multipliers (2.25x and 3x, respectively) and are crucial for transitioning to higher income brackets. While their sprays are more expensive ($1B and $10B), the increased profits justify the investment, especially on valuable mid-tier crops.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Late Game Goal: Rainbow Mutation">
    The **Rainbow mutation** is the ultimate late-game objective, providing a massive 5x income multiplier. Its event chance (1% during Galaxy events) is very low, and the Rainbow Spray costs a staggering $1 trillion. This mutation is best reserved for your most exotic and upgraded crops, as it will generate billions or even trillions per harvest cycle when combined effectively.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info">
Consider using Time Skips strategically. The stronger and more mutated your crops are before activating a time skip, the larger the instant payout will be, especially with high-tier mutations like Rainbow.
</Callout>
