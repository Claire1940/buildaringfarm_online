<Section icon={Target} title="Strategies for Acquiring Mutations">
Obtaining mutations in **Build a Ring Farm** involves a mix of luck with world events and strategic investment in sprays. Understanding the best approach for each stage of the game can help you build your ultimate mutated farm efficiently.

<Callout type="warning" title="Offline Mutation Alert">
Mutations **will not apply** to your plants if you are offline during an event. You must be actively playing the game during world events to have a chance at your plants mutating.
</Callout>

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Monitor World Events">
    Regularly check for ongoing world events like Rain, Blizzard, Black Hole, Nuclear, and Galaxy. These events provide a chance for your crops to spontaneously mutate without any cost. While the chances are low, especially for higher-tier mutations, it's a free opportunity.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Invest in Sprays">
    Sprays are a guaranteed way to apply mutations to your plants. They can be purchased from the Gear Shop. While expensive, they offer reliability, especially for rare mutations. Save your expensive sprays for your most valuable, high-tier crops to maximize their impact.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Target Mutations Based on Progression">
    - **Early Game:** Focus on **Wet mutation** due to its affordability and relatively high event chance.
    - **Mid Game:** Start aiming for **Void** or **Radioactive mutations** once you have a stable income, as their multipliers offer significant boosts.
    - **Late Game:** Dedicate resources to obtaining **Rainbow mutation** sprays or participating in Galaxy Events. This 5x multiplier on high-value crops can generate absurd amounts of money.
  </Step>
</Steps>
