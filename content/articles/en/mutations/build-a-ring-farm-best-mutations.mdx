<Section icon={Target} title="Strategies for Acquiring Best Mutations">
Acquiring the best mutations, especially the coveted Rainbow mutation, requires a strategic approach. You can either rely on world events or invest in sprays.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Monitor World Events">
    World events like Rain, Blizzard, Black Hole, Nuclear, and Galaxy provide a chance for your crops to mutate naturally. Keep an eye on the in-game notifications for these events and ensure you are online during their occurrence. The rarer the mutation, the lower the event chance.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Utilize Gear Shop Sprays">
    Sprays purchased from the Gear Shop guarantee a specific mutation on a chosen plant. While expensive, sprays offer a reliable way to get a mutation without relying on RNG. Save high-tier sprays for your most valuable crops.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Combine Events and Sprays">
    For faster and more consistent mutation acquisition, especially for rarer types, try to combine both methods. Participate in events for a chance, and use sprays on critical crops when events are not favorable or when you need a guaranteed mutation.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Prioritize High-Value Crops">
    Always apply your most powerful mutations to crops that already yield high profits. For example, a Rainbow mutation on a Golden Apple or Kiwi crop can generate billions per harvest cycle, significantly outperforming the same mutation on a basic crop.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info">
The **Acid Spray**, available for $1 Million in the Gear Shop, can be used to remove unwanted mutations from your plants. This is useful if you accidentally apply a low-tier mutation to a high-value crop or want to re-roll for a better one.
</Callout>
