<Section icon={Settings} title="Best PS5 Settings for Performance">

To ensure your **build a ring farm ps5 controls** feel responsive, you must optimize your in-game settings. High-density farms with hundreds of moving parts can cause frame rate drops even on modern consoles. Adjusting the graphics and sensitivity will make a noticeable difference in your reaction time.

<CardGrid cols={3}>
  <Card title="Graphics Mode">
    Switch from **Automatic** to **Manual**. Lower the slider to 2 or 3 if you experience lag in crowded lobbies.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Camera Sensitivity">
    Set this to the **Middle Range**. High sensitivity makes it difficult to precisely target plots with the cursor.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Vibration">
    **Turn Off** vibration. Constant haptic feedback during harvesting can be distracting and drain controller battery.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

<Callout type="warning" title="Lag Reduction">
If the game feels sluggish, enable **Reduce Motion** in the settings. This removes unnecessary animations that can clutter the screen and slow down the PS5's rendering process.
</Callout>

| Setting | Recommended Value | Impact on Gameplay |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Graphics Quality** | Level 3-5 | High FPS stability |
| **Camera Invert** | Off | Standard control feel |
| **Chat Translation** | On | Better global communication |
| **Volume Control** | 50% | Balanced audio cues |
