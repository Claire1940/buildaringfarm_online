<Section icon={Gem} title="Advanced Farming: Mutations, Rings, and Events">
As your farm grows, you'll want to shift your focus towards more advanced strategies like mutation farming, optimizing ring upgrades, and leveraging in-game events. These elements are key to unlocking exponential income growth and acquiring the highest-value seeds.

### Mutation Farming and Event Optimization
Mutations can drastically increase the value of your crops. By understanding how to trigger and stack mutations, you can transform common seeds into highly profitable ones. In-game events often provide temporary boosts that are perfect for accelerating this process.

| Strategy         | Description | Key Benefit |
|:-----------------|:------------|:------------|
| **Stack Mutation Sources** | Use items or upgrades that increase mutation chances. | Higher probability of rare mutations. |
| **Mutation-Ready Seeds** | Keep your main rings loaded with seeds that have good mutation potential. | Maximize profit from event boosts. |
| **Leverage Events** | Plan your farming around active mutation or rain events. | Faster rarity upgrades and increased yield. |
| **Shift to Higher Value Seeds** | Once income is stable, transition to more profitable mutated seeds. | Significant income scaling. |

<Callout type="info">
Rain events are particularly beneficial for mutations and accelerated growth. Always try to capitalize on these periods to maximize your returns.
</Callout>

### Strategic Ring Upgrades
Your farm's "rings" provide crucial multipliers. Upgrading them strategically, especially the outer rings, can lead to massive income boosts.

| Ring Type | Upgrade Priority | Impact |
|:----------|:-----------------|:-------|
| **Outer Rings** | High (First) | Provide the best multiplier gains. |
| **Middle Rings** | Medium | Keep profitable during transition to higher rarity seeds. |
| **Inner Rings** | Lower | Focus on filling with high-value seeds once other rings are optimized. |

<Callout type="tip">
Optimize ring placement for event bonuses. Some events might favor certain ring positions, so adjust your layout accordingly.
</Callout>
