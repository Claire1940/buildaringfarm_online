<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Starting Your Farm: Initial Steps and Upgrades">
Once you've loaded into your plot, the journey to a thriving farm begins. Focus on efficient resource management and smart upgrades to maximize your income.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Select Your Plot">
    Upon entering the game, you'll be prompted to select an available plot. Use your **touchpad and D-pad** to navigate the menu and **Square (X)** to confirm your plot selection. This will be the foundation of your farming empire.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Buy and Plant Your First Seeds">
    Head to the seed shop. Use **Square (X)** to buy a basic seed. Once you have a seed, select an empty spot on your plot, and press **Square (X)** again to plant it. Watch your first crop begin to grow!
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Harvest and Sell Crops">
    When your crops are fully grown, approach them and press **Square (X)** to pick them up. Then, proceed to the selling point (usually a crate or designated area) and press **Square (X)** again to sell your harvest for money.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Upgrade Your Farm">
    Reinvest your earnings. Use the **R2 button** to upgrade existing plants or purchase new, higher-value seeds. Upgrading your plot level will also unlock more planting space, which is crucial for scaling your operation.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Efficient Early Game">
Prioritize upgrading your first seed to at least level 20 quickly. This boosts initial income and allows you to unlock better plots and tools faster.
</Callout>
