### Seed Rolling and Rarity Tiers

Seed rolling is your gateway to higher-earning plants. The more money you invest, the better your chances of getting lucrative seeds.

| Seed Rarity | Example Seeds | Base Value (Approx.) | Strategy |
| :---------- | :------------ | :------------------- | :------- |
| **Common**  | Carrot, Beetroot | 8-10                | Early game staple, phase out quickly |
| **Uncommon** | Onion, Cantaloupe | 77-150              | First major upgrade, focus on leveling |
| **Rare**    | Watermelon, Grape | 300-1,000             | Significant income boost, aim for multiple |
| **Epic**    | Corn, Peach     | 1,800-10,000          | Start saving for these, high ROI |
| **Legendary** | Banana, Mushroom | 100,000+            | Game-changer, requires substantial investment |

<Callout type="warning" title="Reroll Caution">
Be mindful when rerolling seeds. High-tier seeds like Epics and Legendaries can be extremely expensive to purchase if you reroll them too early without sufficient funds. Only reroll if you are certain you can afford to buy the new seed.
</Callout>

### Optimizing Your Farm Layout and Upgrades

Expanding your farm and ensuring your tools keep up with your plant's growth are crucial. The saw range and sprinkler range become increasingly important as you acquire more plots.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Acquire Uncommon/Rare Seeds">
    Continuously roll for seeds. Prioritize getting your first **Onion** or **Watermelon** seed. Once acquired, upgrade it to level 10-15 to maximize its potential.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Expand Plots Strategically">
    Unlock new plots as soon as you can afford them. Fill them with your highest-value seeds. Remember that expanding your plot costs money, but it increases your overall earning potential dramatically.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Upgrade Saw & Sprinkler Range">
    As your farm grows, your saw and sprinkler need to cover more ground. Invest in **Saw Range** and **Sprinkler Range** upgrades to ensure all your plants are harvested and watered efficiently. A common pitfall is having high-level plants that aren't being harvested due to insufficient range.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Increase Seed Rolls">
    Once you have a stable income, upgrade your **Seed Rolls** to get multiple seed options at once. This significantly speeds up the process of finding better seeds.
  </Step>
</Steps>
