Accessing the settings menu is straightforward. Press the **Options button** on your controller, then navigate to the settings icon (usually in the top left). The touchpad can also be used to select settings directly on screen.

### Recommended Settings Adjustments

| Setting Category | Recommended PS4 Adjustment                                | Benefit                                                              |
| :--------------- | :-------------------------------------------------------- | :------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Graphics Mode** | Manual & Low (if lagging) / Auto (if stable)              | Reduces lag, improves frame rate, smoother gameplay.                 |
| **Vibration**    | Off                                                       | Prevents controller distraction, saves battery.                      |
| **Reduce Motion** | On (if lagging)                                           | Helps stabilize performance during intense activity.                 |
| **Camera Invert** | Off (standard)                                            | Maintains intuitive camera controls; avoid if not accustomed to inverted. |
| **Sensitivity**  | Mid-range (adjust to preference)                          | Balances responsive camera movement with precise aiming/selection.   |
| **Volume**       | Adjust to preference                                      | Enhances immersion or allows for background audio.                   |
| **Chat Language** | Set to preferred language                                 | Improves communication with other players.                           |

<Callout type="success" title="Performance Boost">
If you experience frequent lag or frame rate drops, immediately switch your Graphics Mode to "Manual" and lower the settings. Additionally, enabling "Reduce Motion" can provide a noticeable performance improvement.
</Callout>
