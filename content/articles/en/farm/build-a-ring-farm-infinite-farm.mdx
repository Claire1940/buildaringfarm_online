### The Automation Process

The game's automated systems handle watering and harvesting without player intervention once placed and upgraded. Your role shifts from manual labor to strategic management and continuous upgrading.

1.  **Planting**: Manually place seeds on unlocked plots.
2.  **Watering**: Sprinklers automatically water plants within their range. Upgrading sprinkler power speeds this up.
3.  **Growing**: Plants grow over time. Faster growth means faster income.
4.  **Harvesting**: Automated choppers (saws) harvest fully grown plants within their range. Upgrading saw range covers more area.
5.  **Selling**: Harvested produce is automatically sold, and money is added to your balance.
