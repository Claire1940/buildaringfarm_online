### Strategies for Acquiring Mutations

There are two primary methods to get mutations on your plants: through random in-game events or by purchasing and using sprays from the Gear Shop.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Monitor In-Game Events">
    Keep an eye on the in-game event timer. Events like Rain, Blizzard, Black Hole, Nuclear, and Galaxy offer a percentage chance for your plants to mutate. You *must* be online during these events for the mutation chance to activate.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Utilize Gear Shop Sprays">
    For a more direct approach, you can purchase specific mutation sprays from the Gear Shop. While expensive, these sprays guarantee the application of a particular mutation to your chosen plant. This is especially useful for targeting the high-tier mutations like Rainbow once you have accumulated enough wealth.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Strategic Mutation Removal">
    If a plant receives an undesirable mutation, you can remove it using an **Acid Spray**, available for $1M from the Gear Shop. This allows you to re-attempt for a better mutation, especially on high-rarity seeds.
  </Step>
</Steps>
