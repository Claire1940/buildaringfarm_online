<Section icon={Shield} title="Optimizing Plant Growth and Income with Upgrades">
To maximize your earnings in **Build a Ring Farm**, it's not enough to just plant high-rarity seeds; you need to optimize their growth and yield through various upgrades. Sprinklers and Saw Yield are two critical components that significantly enhance your farm's productivity.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Unlock and Upgrade Sprinklers">
    Sprinklers reduce the time each plant stage takes to grow. Invest in both **Sprinkler Power** and **Sprinkler Range** to cover more plots and accelerate growth across your farm. A wider range ensures all your plants benefit from the speed boost.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Enhance Saw Yield">
    Saw Yield directly increases the number of units harvested per plant. Upgrading this ensures that each harvest from your valuable plants brings in significantly more money. This is especially impactful for high-rarity seeds.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Strategic Plant Placement">
    Utilize the ring multipliers for optimal placement. The **Base Ring** offers 7x, the **Middle Ring** 13x, and the **Outer Ring** provides a massive 19x multiplier. Place your most valuable and highly mutated plants in the outer rings.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Continuous Seed Rolls">
    Keep rolling for new seeds using the lever. While it might seem random, consistent rolls increase your chances of acquiring rare, secret, and even prismatic seeds. Remember, the rarer the seed, the higher the potential income.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Upgrade Type | Benefit                                | Cost Impact (Initial) | Long-Term Value         |
| :----------- | :------------------------------------- | :-------------------- | :---------------------- |
| **Sprinkler Power** | Faster plant growth                    | Moderate              | High (consistent income) |
| **Sprinkler Range** | Covers more plots, efficient watering  | Moderate              | High (farm-wide effect) |
| **Saw Yield** | Increased units per harvest            | High                  | **Very High** (direct profit) |
| **Seed Luck** | Higher chance for rare seeds           | Moderate              | Medium (acquisition aid) |
| **Seed Rolls** | More attempts to get new seeds         | Low                   | Medium (diversification) |
